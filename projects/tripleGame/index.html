Triple Game

Pong wasn't hard to recreate, I made the ball bounce by flipping the speed from postive to negative and vice versa. You are able to move the backboard up and not nothing else, nor is there an real ending you just quit when you get bored.

Arkanoid had some issues, this was with the the way the ball would bounce back in the correct way if it hit something. I was stuck on this for a while but I managed to figure it out.

The platformer was fun to map out and try to beat it. I made it too hard for myself I could never beat it myself without savestates due to how little control you have over the player once you jump. The only controls are to move left and right and to jump.
